Mission
We help teens navigate through their emotions, one step at a time. We provide teens with a free, high-quality wellness app. Our accessible resource is powered with an AI emotion detector and a forum designed specifically for teens, by teens.
Features
AI Mood Tracker
SimplyJoy has a powerful AI mood tracker that scans your face for emotions. It analyzes your facial expression to help you figure out what you're feeling. Plus, you can even keep track of those emotions in a minimimalistic, easy-to-use system.
Journal
An online journal to help you record your thoughts and emotions. Express your thoughts freely with a blank template, or use one of our prompts to help you get started.
Forums
SimplyJoy also has forums for you to discuss mental health issues with other teens like you. It is a safe community to talk and relate to each other.
Free
SimplyJoy is completely, 100% free. It is also specifically designed for teens, by teens.

Frequently Asked Questions
How do I download SimplyJoy?
Search up "SimplyJoy" in the iOS App Store, or just click on the button at the top of the website, which will take you directly to the App Store.
Do I need to create an account?
Yes. An account will keep track of all the information you input, and it helps with safety to ensure that all SimplyJoy users come from a safe background. Especially due to the forums, background checks are extremely important.
What if I don't want to use the AI part of the mood tracker?
No problem! There is an option below the AI emotion recognition that allows you to manually input a mood. Any time you feel that you want to record a mood yourself, just use that option to do so.
What is the cost? Are there any in-app purchases?
SimplyJoy is 100% free for any teen to use. There are no in-app purchases, as we believe that resources for mental health should be equal and accessible for everyone.
